About us

"Adopt don't shop!" is a movement encouraging the adoption of pets from shelters or rescues instead of purchasing them from breeders or pet stores.

Adopting a dog from a shelter saves lives by reducing overcrowding in shelters, combats irresponsible breeding and illegal puppy mills
